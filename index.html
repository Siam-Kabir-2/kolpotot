About the Club

Kolpopot is the artists' society of KUET. It is a creative club that works with art, exhibitions, competitions, and cultural activities. The club gives students a platform to show their creativity and participate in artistic events.

Club Activities

The club is involved in the following activities:

  1. Organizing art exhibitions
  2. Collecting artworks from students
  3. Running art competitions
  4. Participating in university club fairs
  5. Promoting student creativity through social media
